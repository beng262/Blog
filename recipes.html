Delicious Pancakes Recipe

Recipes 2025-03-26

Learn how to make delicious, fluffy pancakes with this easy recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Pour batter onto a hot griddle.
  3. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  4. Serve with syrup and fruit.

Step-by-Step Checklist:

  • Gather ingredients
  • Mix ingredients thoroughly
  • Preheat the griddle
  • Pour and cook batter
  • Plate and serve

Classic Baklava Recipe

Recipes 2025-04-01

Enjoy this classic baklava with layers of filo, nuts, and honey syrup.

Ingredients:

  • Filo pastry
  • Mixed nuts
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Honey

Instructions:

  1. Layer filo with butter and nuts.
  2. Bake until golden and crispy.
  3. Drizzle with honey syrup while warm.

Step-by-Step Checklist:

  • Prepare nuts and filo
  • Layer ingredients
  • Bake until crisp
  • Drizzle honey syrup
